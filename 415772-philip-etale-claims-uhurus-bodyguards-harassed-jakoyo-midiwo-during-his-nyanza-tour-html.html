    Orange Democratic Movement (ODM) director of communications Philip Etale has sensationally claimed President Uhuru Kenyatta's bodyguards harassed the late former Gem member of parliament Jakoyo Midiwo during his Nyanza tour.

    In a lengthy Facebook post on Tuesday, June 15, Etale narrated how the former ODM lawmaker was pushed and shoved by the hawked eyed security men on Sunday, May 30.

    The Orange party official said Midiwo was manhandled in broad daylight when the president commissioned the Yala-Bondo Water Supply Plant in Gem Constituency.

    Midiwo blocked, shoved

    Etale said trouble started when the president arrived at the venue to launch the water facility when Midiwo was blocked by Uhuru's bodyguards at the gate.

    "The president was accompanied by my boss Raila Odinga. When he arrived, Hon Jakoyo Midiwo who was in the president’s motorcade alighted from his vehicle and walked towards the plant. The president’s security detail at the main gate to the plant deliberately stopped him.""They pushed and shoved him as he pleaded with them, and in fact explaining to them that he is the immediate former MP for the constituency. The adamant security officers could hear nothing. Midiwo was manhandled as I watched," Etale recalled.

    The 2022 MP aspirant said he confronted the officers but his pleas fell on deaf ears.

    "I asked why they were harassing Hon Midiwo, the man responded “nimesema haingii hapa” ( I have said he can't get in here). I asked him again...this is Hon Midiwo, you mean you don’t know him? At this point, he looked at me and pushed him (Midiwo) away as if saying “munaniudhi” (You are annoying me). Members of the media were present and can attest to this."

    Etale said Midiwo was only allowed in after Embakasi East MP Babu Owino intervened.

    At that point, he noted the late MP looked 'dejected and disturbed' by the turn of events.

    Midiwo's death

    Midiwo died on June 14 according to a tweet posted by Etale.

    The ODM official asked Kenyans to pray for the family and friends of the politician who passed on after a long illness.

    The ODM party also confirmed the news of Midiwo's demise and eulogised him as a gallant son of the soil and a defender of the party who never shied away from speaking the truth.

    "Sad day for our party. We have just lost a gallant son of the soil, a founder member of the Change Movement and a three-time Member of Parliament for Gem Hon. Jakoyo Midiwo. "He was a defender of the party and never shied away from speaking the truth. To the family, we say pole sana," the statement from ODM indicated.

    Uhuru eulogised him as a straight-shooting debater on the floor of parliament who became famous for witty anecdotes drawn from his interactions with his constituents.
