    'RHUGT: Ex-Wives Club' Will Air on Bravo — Find Out When!

    By Elizabeth Randolph

    Oct. 4 2022, Updated 1:57 p.m. ET

    After a successful first season, The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip, returned on Peacock in the summer of 2022 for its latest installment: Ex-Wives Club. RHUGT brings cast members from different franchises together for a vacation — though the trip is never relaxing.

    The debut season of the show followed longtime, active Housewives cast members. For the sophomore season, the format switched up, and the drama was never-ending.

    In Season 2, RHOA all-stars Phaedra Parks and Eva Marcille Sterling returned back to the small screen. Additionally, other former Bravo stars — Vicki Gunvalson, Tamra Judge, Jill Zarin, and Taylor Armstrong — gathered at Dorinda Medley’s Berkshires estate, Blue Stone Manor, to live under one roof for eight days.

    While RHUGT Season 2 highlights past Housewives, the show didn't debut on the same network that helped make its cast famous. However, much to the delight of non-Peacock subscribers, the series will air on Bravo sooner rather than later.

    So, will 'RHUGT: Ex-Wives Club' air on Bravo?

    Unlike other shows within the Housewives franchise, RHUGT is a part of Peacock’s original programming. Nonetheless, the service provides love to all of Bravo’s series, becoming its official streaming home in May 2022.

    RHUGT: Ex-Wives Club exclusively premiered on Peacock, but fans had reason to hope that the show would, at some point, air on Bravo.

    Though shows like RHUGT, RHOM, and Below Deck Down Under premiered exclusively on Peacock, each show has gone on to air on Bravo several months later.

    When Season 2 of RHUGT first premiered on June 23, 2022, there was no official confirmation about a potential Bravo air date.

    The second season will kick off on the network on Friday, Oct. 14 at 8 p.m. ET.

    Viewers will soon be able to find out how the former Real Housewives fared when they spent time in the Berkshires. Plus, as fans already know, Tamra Judge and Taylor Armstrong will soon be returning to the network when they appear on the upcoming 17th season of RHOC.

    How many episodes of ‘RHUGT: Ex-Wives Club’ are there?

    The entire season of RHUGT: Ex-Wives Club will last for seven episodes. All of the Season 2 episodes are available to stream on Peacock now, but fans can expect to also see the episodes air on Bravo on Fridays.

    The entire second season was filmed at Dorinda's manor, which RHONY fans became familiar with during her time on the show.

    "I got lucky because Blue Stone Manor is such a beloved character already... for the audience to be able to see Blue Stone Manor in a different way, with all these superstar girls on it, I think is gonna be very exciting for the audience," Dorinda said during an episode of ​​her SiriusXM podcast Make It Nice.

    How exactly did the ladies fare when they spent the week at Dorinda's infamous home? You'll have to tune in to find out.

    You can stream the first two seasons of RHUGT on Peacock now.
