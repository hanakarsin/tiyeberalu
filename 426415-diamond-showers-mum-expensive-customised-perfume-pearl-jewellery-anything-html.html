    Diamond Showers Mum With Expensive Customised Perfume, Pearl Jewellery: Anything for Her

    Bongo star Diamond Platnumz has showered his mother, Mama Dangote, with expensive gifts that certainly made her proud. The singer went on his Instagram sharing a video of his mum seated on the floor while unboxing her gifts, with a huge smile on her face.

    11 May 2024, 00:00
    • Diamond gifted his mum several pearl jewellery and designer perfumes on Wednesday
    • Mama Dangote was lost for words as she unpacked her gifts, noting she felt very confused
    • The singer said he would give his mother anything, and she reciprocated by thanking him for the gifts

    Bongo star Diamond Platnumz has showered his mother, Mama Dangote, with expensive gifts that certainly made her proud.

    The singer went on his Instagram sharing a video of his mum seated on the floor while unboxing her gifts, with a huge smile on her face.

    The gifts included Miss Dior designer perfumes, which had her name (Mama Dangote) inscribed at the bottom of the bottle. She even brought one closer to the camera to show how nice it looked.

    The proud mum also opened up boxes containing beautiful pearl jewellery gifts that included necklaces, bracelets, earrings and even a gold plated watch with pearls on the wrists.

    At one point, the mum could be heard chuckling as she noted her confusion towards which gift to open first.

    Diamond held the ribbon showing that it also had his mother's name engraved on it.

    In a small caption, the Jeje hitmaker wrote, "anything for her", showing his love for his treasured mama.

    Mama Dangote also went on her Instagram Stories to thank her son for the gifts.

    Diamond and his mother share a unique bond, having been raised by a single mum who was always there for him.
