    How to arrange a bedsitter

    How to arrange a bedsitter is very simple and easy. Still doubting? Well, this article will give you only 10 tips that will make your bedsitter aesthetically appealing to both you and your visitors. The tips will as well make it appear stylish and give you a comfortable home. Read on how to arrange a bedsitter in Kenya with images accompanying each tip to act as an inspiration for you.

    1. Purchase room dividers

    Do you want to know how to make a bedsitter to look spacious? Well, purchase the room dividers. The reason as to why you should purchase dividers is that they make your room to give an impression of having numerous rooms. The demarcate sections include the living room, the bedroom, and the kitchen area. We bet that your bedsitter will have that beautiful and amazing look. You can just imagine how spacious your small palace will look like.

    What can you use to divide your room? Well, most Kenyans go for the curtain room dividers. You can also use shelves, gallery wall, or maybe cabinets to do the dividing of the room. If you go for the shelves, kindly arrange it properly with staffs that suit it such as books. Most go for the gallery wall because cardboards are available at a cheaper price in Kenya; your favorite pictures on the cardboard will also make your room glow.

    2. Use coloured light

    The coloured lights will definitely create a beautiful ambiance in your bedsitter. That bright colour makes a room to pop making it to attract you and anyone who gives you a visit. Try it and you will fall in love with the illusions that the bright colours create in your small palace.

    The beautiful thing about lighting a room with the colored lights is that they are very powerful. You can purchase the color changing bulbs that will give you a dozen of color shades to light up your bedsitter. The bulbs are available in different formats and creativity, the more cheaper the color-changing LED bulb is, the more it has limited colors to choose from. You can also purchase one colored LED bulb that has your color palette to save on your cash.

    During the day, it is best that you allow sunlight into the room. This means that you should avoid curtains that are too heavy because they are opaque and they will not brighten the room. You can go for the sheer curtains and experience how they lit up the room’s mood. Yes, the heavy curtains create a beautiful style in a room, but they block off the light in your bedsitter making them not to be a good choice for a one-room unit.

    3. Choice of furniture

    How to arrange a small bedsitter will be simple if you consider the type of furniture you purchase. A bedsitter is probably a room that can best accommodate one person. Therefore, there is no harm if you go for the multi –purpose furniture especially if your bedsitter has a limited space. Cluttering the room with loads of furniture will makes it look awful. For instance, you can mount shelves on the room’s wall or go for the under-bed drawers. You can also purchase a table or seat with drawers.

Some even prefer to go for a sofa bed and their bedsitters look fabulous. It is very vital for you to always pay attention to the size of furniture before you purchase it.     4. Try as much as possible to create illusions

    What most people do, they use mirrors to create illusions about the size of the room. A mirror will make your room appear more bigger.

    Some even go to an extent of going for furniture with high legs. This is because the exposed floor area create illusions that a room is much bigger than it actually is. The low piece of furniture will always make a room appear crowded and this is where many Kenyans go wrong. Another solution to create illusions is to go for glass as well as metallic furniture such as a table.

    5. Keep the room clean and tidy always

    An untidy room will forever appear cluttered and therefore you should always try to be neat and tidy. To make it easier for you, you can put things that you use more often at an easy reach and isolate the ones that are no longer necessary. If you have a shelf, you can arrange some of your staffs there in an organized manner. In simple words, try as much as you can to make the room clean such as making the beddings. A neatly arranged bedsitter looks elegant at all time.

    All this does not necessarily means that you should clean it everyday, but you can make time and clean it like twice a week. Put things at their right places; use something and return it to its right place and you will definitely not have a tiresome time cleaning it.

    The kitchenette should always be organized. What most people do is to purchase big lunch boxes as well as dish racks to store food. Your dishes should always be clean to avoid insects; it is a small room and keeping it clean will make you comfortable.

    6. Have a colour theme

    Decorating a tiny bedsitter with few colour themes will make it look fabulous. What are your favorite colors? Well, try to stick to your colour palette. For instance if you go for white and black theme, then avoid mixing it up with other colours. In this way, you will make your small palace have a calm look and not complicated as well as cluttered.

    Something else you should pay attention to, is the choice of your colors for the room. You should always go for pale colours such as blues, white, pale greys, as well as cream. This is because they tend to lit the room; making it appear airy.

    7. Have creative ideas

    The best to arrange a bedsitter, is adding a little creative ideas to make your small palace have an awesome look. Everybody is in love with a unique look, different style and mood. For instance, you can mount a piece of wood on the room using an old belt and we bet the room will have an awesome view.

    8. Purchase accessories

    Accessories makes a room to be colourful especially when you pimp it with your own style. Most people with small bedsitter designs tend to think that they cant pimp it, which is not the case because the size does not necessarily matter. You can place lamps as well as bold arts at different spots of your house.

    Many prefer a gallery wall art and they work miracle on the looks of a bedsitter. The good bit about a gallery wall art is that it will also act as a room divider. You can pimp a cardboard with your favorite quality photos and we bet you won’t regret the efforts. Colorful pillows are also awesome in adding a little personal touch in a room.

    9. Use colourful rugs

    Colorful rugs are easy to spot in the streets and they are also very affordable in Kenya. You can also use some of your colorful old clothes especially T-shirts as rugs. Why colorful? Well, it makes a room to have a homely, elegant, as well as warmly look. Apart from that, the rugs will allow you to walk barefoot as they are soft and warm; bare cement is too cold.
