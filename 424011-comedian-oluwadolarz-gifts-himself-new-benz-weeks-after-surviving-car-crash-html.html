    Nigerian comedian, Oluwadolarz recently took to social media to share the news he has bought a brand new Mercedes Benz with fans.

    Taking to Instagram, the funny man shared a photo of the new ride as he posed beside it.

    The automobile seemed to be the comedian’s gift to himself since the white car was decorated with a red bow.

    However, Oluwadolarz did not share more details about the Benz in the caption of his post, but fans celebrated him regardless.
