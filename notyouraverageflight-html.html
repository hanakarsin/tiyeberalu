    YouTube gamer and basketball enthusiast known for his exciting, energetic and entertaining videos pertaining to real-life NBA action as well as the video games NBA 2K and NBA Live. He has earned over 4.2 million subscribers to his NotYourAverageFlight channel. He also posts his original music and clips of himself playing basketball to an additional YouTube channel titled FlightReacts. 

    Before Fame

    He worked two jobs in the fast food industry. 

    Trivia

    He started YouTube as a means to relieve himself of depression and anger. He partnered with EA Sports under their game changer program. 

    Family Life

    He was born Kimani White in Washington D.C and then moved to West Palm Beach, Florida where he would grow up. He dated Ariel Diaz and the two briefly ran a YouTube channel together. He has also dated rapper Ti Taylor from the Taylor Girlz. On March 2nd, 2021 he welcomed his first child, a daughter named Jae June.

    Associated With

    Several of his videos' thumbnails feature NBA superstars such as LeBron James, Kevin Durant, Kobe Bryant and more.
