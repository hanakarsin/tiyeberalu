    Elite is a Spanish teen drama that airs on Netflix. The series follows a group of students at Las Encinas, a reputable private school. The storyline delves deep into their complicated personal lives involving love, sexuality, abuse, social class, and other teenage anxieties.

    The dramatic events are all set against a background of murder incidents that have rocked the school community.
