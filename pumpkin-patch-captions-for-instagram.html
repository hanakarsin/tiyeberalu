    Pumpkin Patch Captions for Instagram — 40 Fun Captions for Your Next Post!

    By Pippa Raga

    Updated Oct. 25 2022, 4:33 p.m. ET

    It’s that time of the year again: the season for colorful foliage, crisp air, oversized sweaters, and of course, pumpkins. Between jack-o'-lanterns, pumpkin patches, pumpkin spice lattes, and mouthwatering pumpkin pies, pumpkins are obviously the unofficial mascot of fall.
