    Carol Radull Thanks God after 'Healing' from Side Effects of COVID-19 Vaccine

    Kenya’s Pharmacy and Poisons Board (PPB) on Wednesday, April 7, announced that 279 people had reported suffering from adverse effects after taking the Oxford-AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine.

    Well, since then, there have been debates on whether the vaccines indeed have side effects or word going round is just that; rumours.

    Sports journalist Carol Radull has put the debate to rest after confirming she has been dealing with terrible side effects after receiving the COVID-19 vaccine.

    Radull did not indicate which vaccine she took but confirmed the side effects put her down.

    According to the journalist, the side effects took a toll on her for two days.

    The Kiss 100 radio presenter confirmed she had a terrible fever for two days.

    ''So the side effects of the Covid vaccine hit me so hard that I've been "out" for 2 days. I haven't had a fever like that since I had malaria in high school.'' Radull wrote.

    Radull informed her fans and followers she was feeling better as she thanked everyone who checked up on her when she was down.

    ''Thanks to everyone who checked up on me. If I didn't pick your call or answer your texts; now you know why. Much love. Feeling better now and will host Radull Live at 1.00pm. At least "Great" Roy is a ball of energy Roy Karuhize. See you then ❤ Only GOD '' she added.
