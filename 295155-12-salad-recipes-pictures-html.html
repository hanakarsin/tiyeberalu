    Compared to fruits which have always been considered to be beneficial to your health, vegetables are equally essential due to their high vitamin content as well as the essential minerals necessary for boosting the body energy levels. Moreover, they have a high fiber content which improves bowel movement and eases the digestion process. Due to the fact that they contain a high water content alongside low calories, vegetable and fruit salads can be the best choice if you are hoping to shed off some additional weight this new year. Additionally, if you regularly consume bright colored vegetables, then you stand to benefit from a supply of antioxidants that eliminate dandruff and give a smooth skin. Having the best salad recipes at your disposal will make it easy to incorporate vegetables into your diet.

    Which salad varieties are the healthiest and easy to prepare with locally available ingredients? If you are looking for answers to this popular question, then this compilation of the best salad recipes will guide you on how to prepare delicious salad meals from on-season fruits and fresh vegetables that are cheaply available.

    What are some good salads to make?

    Here are some incredible recipes to help you shake up your lunch routine and live a healthy life.

    1. The Kenyan Kachumbari (the most popular salad recipe in Kenya)

    This is usually a light salad meal that is made from onions and tomatoes. It can serve as the ideal accompaniment for grilled or roasted meat as well as various rice dishes. Looking to learn how you can prepare this simple salad meal? Here’s how to go about the 15-minute delicacy.

    Kachumbari salad ingredients (Serving 5)

    • Three large and ripe tomatoes
    • Two medium sized onions
    • A medium-large sized avocado
    • A small green or red diced chili
    • A tablespoonful of lemon extract
    • A tablespoonful of coriander (ensure that it is freshly chopped)

    Preparation method

    As mentioned earlier, this is a pretty simple recipe. Here are the preparation steps:

  • Place all your diced onions in a container (preferably a bowl) and cover it with saline water. You can let the mixture sit for approximately 10 minutes
  • Add all the diced tomatoes, chili, and avocado to one large bowl
  • Drain off all the water in the onions bowl then add them to the bowl with the rest of the ingredients
  • Finally, add some salt as well as the lemon extract and mix thoroughly

    2. Kale salad

    This is appropriate if you are in a weight loss program and trying to figure out a new salad to try out. It features a simple lemon dressing (homemade), which makes it the ideal light lunch or healthy side dish. Additionally, it is delicious.

    Here’s the preparation guide for the 25-minute serving for four.

    Ingredients

    • Four cups of kales (shredded)
    • A cup of chickpeas (cooked)
    • A cup of black olives (sliced)
    • A cup of chopped tomatoes
    • Six tablespoons of sunflower seeds (toasted)
    • A teaspoonful of Dijon mustard
    • A teaspoonful of black pepper
    • Two teaspoons of minced garlic
    • Four tablespoons of olive oil
    • Six tablespoons of lemon juice
    • A cup of grated cheese
    • Salt

    Preparation instructions

  • Place your shredded kale, chickpeas, sunflower seeds, olives, and tomatoes in a huge bowl then mix well
  • Mix the olive oil, black pepper, lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, and a little salt to prepare the dressing
  • Add this dressing to the vegetable bowl then toss
  • Sprinkle your cheese on the mixture then serve

    3. Kale salad with apples

    To get it right with this type, ensure that you use the most tender kale you then chop them very finely or cut them into thin filaments. Here’s how you can prepare the delicacy in five minutes.

    Ingredients

    • Four cups of finely chopped kale
    • Two apples
    • Two tablespoons of lemon juice
    • Two tablespoons of toasted almonds (coarsely chopped)
    • 5 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil

    Preparation steps

  • Combine the apple, kale, and almonds in a huge bowl
  • Whisk the lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, and salt in a separate bowl to create the dressing
  • Add the mixture to the salad before tossing it well and serving

    • Note that this type will come out perfect if you toss with the dressing approximately 15 minutes before it is served. The kales will soften when mixed with the dressing

    4. Tomato and lettuce vegetable salad

    This delicacy is all about simplicity as it is basically a flat salad, and most prefer it to bowl salads since the dressing used perfectly complements these vegetables as opposed to suffocating them. You need to ensure that the tomatoes are ripe enough (for the sweet taste) and you can cut them in wages or any form you like.

    The preparation takes 15 minutes, and here is a guide on how you can make the salad for four.

    Ingredients

    • A cup of lettuce (chopped, of course)
    • Two medium sized tomatoes (sliced)
    • Two medium sized onions (also sliced)
    • A medium-sized avocado that is cut into pieces
    • A cup of yellow or green capsicum
    • Two medium sized carrots (grated)
    • A pinch of salt
    • Olive oil

    How to prepare it

  • Place all your sliced onions in salty water, allowing them to stand for about ten minutes before finally draining the water.
  • Add the onions, avocado, tomatoes, capsicum, lettuce, and carrot in a huge bowl then add the salt and toss several times to mix them
  • Finish it up by dressing the mixture with olive oil

    • 5. Healthy local fruit salad

    This salad is not only delicious, but also quite nutritious. The delicacy contains loads of magnesium, potassium, folate, copper, as well as vitamins K, C, and A. Fruits such as pawpaw, contain enzymes known to support the digestive process, while others such as avocado offer beneficial monounsaturated fats which balance the sugars in other fruits.

    Here’s the preparation guide for a serving of four.

    Ingredients

    • A medium sized mango (diced)
    • Half medium sized pineapple (diced)
    • Half medium sized pawpaw (also diced)
    • One sizeable beetroot (grated)
    • Three medium sized bananas in circular slices
    • A sliced avocado
    • A quarter of a medium sized watermelon (diced)

    Preparation guide

  • Add all these ingredients into a bowl
  • Mix thoroughly before serving

    • 6. Kenyan cabbage salad

    The simple Kenyan cabbage salad is among the most popular side dishes eaten alongside a range of local delicacies. In fact, most individuals consider it their favorite. One of the reasons why this is among the most preferred dish is because you can adjust it slightly and add a variety of ingredients to match what you specifically want to eat.

    So how do you prepare it the delicacy for four individuals?

    Ingredients

    • Four cups of red and green cabbages respectively (shredded)
    • One cup of already grated carrots
    • Two tablespoons olive oil
    • A tablespoonful of mustard
    • Two tablespoons of sugar (white)
    • A little salt
    • Half a cup of cider vinegar

    How to prepare it

  • Add the mustard, olive oil, white sugar, and cider vinegar into a jar and stir well. This will be used for dressing
  • In a large bowl, place your green cabbage, carrots, red cabbage, and dressing then toss
  • Allow the salad to stand for approximately 20 minutes then serve

    • 7. Spinach, apple, and carrot salad

    The delicacy does not involve a great deal of prep work. Its dressing calls for minced onions and this is precisely the only prep work. Fast things first, here are the ingredients and preparation guidelines for four servings.

    Ingredients

    • A huge grated carrot
    • Two peeled and grated apples
    • A handful of baby spinach (chopped)
    • A tablespoonful of lemon extract
    • A teaspoonful of olive oil
    • A teaspoon of mustard
    • Half a teaspoonful of black pepper
    • Two tablespoons of cider vinegar

    Preparation steps

  • Add the carrots, spinach, and apples in a huge bowl
  • In an additional bowl, add the lemon extract, mustard, and olive oil to create a smooth dressing
  • Combine your dressing and vegetables before seasoning with salt and pepper

    • 8. Tasty tomato, olives, and onion salad

    This delicious salad can be served both as a side dish and as a snack. The preparation may last about 25 minutes, and here is the procedure for preparing four servings.

    Ingredients

    • Eight ripe tomatoes cut into sixteen wedges (cut each in quarters)
    • Two well-sliced onions of medium size
    • A few black olives
    • Four tablespoons of olive oil
    • A teaspoon of black pepper
    • Four tablespoons of vinegar
    • Basil oil
    • A pinch of salt

    How to prepare

    Begin by adding the vinegar and olive oil in a large bowl before seasoning with salt and black pepper

  • Add onions, olives, and tomatoes into this bowl then mix thoroughly
  • Allow the mixture some 20 minutes
  • Add basil oil for that extra flavor and color

    • 9. Mango, beetroot, and cucumber salad

    This coming weekend, you can choose to treat yourself, spouse, friend, or family with this delicious and healthy combination. The preparation is as easy as the previously discussed varieties and it will take you about 10 minutes to come up with an appetizing salad.

    Here is how you can prepare four servings of this sumptuous salad.

    Ingredients

    • Two medium sized cucumbers (sliced)
    • Two medium sized beetroots (grated)
    • A cup of shred lettuce
    • A cup of diced mangoes
    • Two teaspoons of lemon extract

    Going about the preparation

  • Add the beetroot, cucumber, mangoes, and lettuce then mix well
  • Sprinkle your lemon extract then toss

    • 10. Beetroot and avocado salad

    This is a nourishing creation that can best be described as a ‘superfood salad’. It offers a combination of good stuff that would leave you feeling conscientious and healthy at the same time. Here is are the requirements and procedure for preparing this delicacy for four.

    Ingredients

    • Four medium sized beetroots (peeled, chopped, and cooked)
    • Two avocados (medium-sized and chopped)
    • A pair of small onions
    • A tablespoonful of olive oil
    • Two tablespoons of parsley (chopped)
    • A few parsley leaves
    • Two tablespoons of vinegar (white)
    • A teaspoonful of ground cumin
    • Two tablespoons of lemon extract
    • Salt

    How to prepare

  • Place your avocado and beetroot in a single large bowl
  • Add the rest of the ingredients in a smaller bowl then mix them well to get a fine dressing
  • Add this dressing to the avocado/beetroot mixture then toss
  • Garnish with the parsley

    If you’re looking for a salad variety that does not require you to combine the different dressing types, then you should try out the beetroot apple salad. Also easy to prepare, the delicacy comes with a range of nutritional benefits that you can tap and improve your health.

    • 12. Kenya curried cabbage

    This is not different from the previously mentioned cabbage salad. Only a few different ingredients make it different.

    Ingredients

    • One shredded cabbage head
    • Two tbl butter
    • Finely minced white onion
    • One cup of shredded carrots
    • A quarter cup of heavy cream
    • One teaspoon curry powder
    • One teaspoon of salt
    • Half a teaspoon of freshly-ground white pepper

    Preparation guidelines

  • Saute the minced onion into the butter until it turns translucent
  • Add your shredded cabbage as you toss continuously for a few minutes allowing it to cook partially. Sprinkle the white pepper and curry powder over the vegetable and mix well
  • Add the heavy cream and let it reduce for 1-2 minutes.
  • Add salt then stir the shredded carrot before adding the salt and cook on until the carrots get enough heat. Ensure it doesn’t overcook.
  • Serve

    • How can I make salad taste better?

    Ever noticed that the salad you order at a restaurant in a way tastes different and a little bit more special than those which you whip up by yourself at home? If you relate with this scenario, then that will not be the case anymore.

    Here are some tips on how to get it right

    • Prepare the dressing of the salad - Use fresh ingredients that would give the dressing that extra punch of flavor that would be difficult to get in a bottle. The standard ratio is three parts oil combined with one part of vinegar. However, you are free to skew it more if you would like your dressing to be slightly more acidic.
    • Season the greens - One of the biggest secrets of achieving that exceptionally delicious salad is proper seasoning. You can also make a point of additionally seasoning the greens too.
    • Include a variety - A good mixture of greens offers a varied flavor and texture, a tip that chefs use well to elevate their salads. Try this out on your cooking as well if you want to enjoy your homemade salad.
    • Mix with fresh herbs - You can be extra adventurous and include an additional set of fresh herbs as this is the perfect opportunity to utilize whatever herbs you may be having in the house. This will give you the fresh taste required of a well-prepared salad.
    • Do not fear the fat - Just like in restaurants, you shouldn’t be afraid to try adding some oil to the mix since fat equals flavor. This will make it taste better and more satiating. You can add this with the dressing or with ingredients like bacon, toasted nuts, cheese, or avocado.

    Is eating salad everyday good for you?

    Including salad in your diet almost every day may be among the most healthy and recommended eating habits adopted by many across the world. It is also one of the simplest to prepare and a super-convenient way of working in a couple of vegetable or fruit servings.

    Here are some of the health benefits of eating salads

    1. Combine the goodness of vegetables and fruits

    It’s important to note that plant foods that are also nutrient-rich play a significant role when it comes to the overall body health. Also, if you eat green salads frequently, you are likely to have higher levels of powerful antioxidants in the blood which protect you from the dangers of free radicals in the body.

    2. You need the fiber

    It may be hard to believe that foods that cannot even be digested can be so essential for the wellbeing of the digestive system. High-fiber diets lower cholesterol levels as well as help prevent constipation. It also makes you feel fuller AN consume less food, thus significantly contributing to weight loss

    3. Increase satisfaction while cutting on calories

    If you are aiming at losing weight, then it’s advisable that you begin every meal with a green salad. According to studies, consuming a low-calorie diet as the first course will enhance your satiety, thus reducing the number of calories you will eat during the meal.

    4. Benefit from the smart fats

    By eating a small amount of healthy fat (such as those monounsaturated fats in avocado, nuts, and olive oil) alongside the vegetables helps your body to take in protective phytochemicals such as lutein and lycopene.
