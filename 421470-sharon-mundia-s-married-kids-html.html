    Mundia on having kids in future

    Through a video she shared on her YouTube channel, the mother of one answered some of the questions her fans have been sending her that have also caused debate in the past.

    One fan had asked:

    "Do you see marriage in your future? If yes, would you want more kids?

    The content creator then replied:

    "I don't know. I think my relationship with marriage and the construct of marriage has changed quite a bit. I think growing up, I was so excited to get married and specifically, it's the wedding I was excited about.But now, I am more interested in establishing a safe, healthy, mutually beneficial relationship. I am not sure about marriage as I am not married to the idea of marriage."

    The celebrity also said that there have been moments she's had baby fever and desired to have another baby, but then she would tell herself that she would be okay if it was just her and her little girl.

    Her fans also asked her if she was bisexual, and she said:

    "I have really had a hard time answering this question because one I would rather not have people talk about who is in my bed because it's no one's business and secondly i would rather leave mystery because I am okay. I am not a lesbian but I also want to add to that that love is love. We should be less judgemental," she said.

    Mundia talks about relationship with women

    The YouTuber and her ex-husband Lonina Leteipan are currently co-parenting after a nasty breakup in 2017.

    Their engagement and wedding were the talk of the town for days after Lonina romantically proposed to Mundia after a chopper ride to Mt Kenya, and by a lake back in 2015.

    The couple then tied the knot in a lavish wedding ceremony in 2016 but their marriage didn't last and rumours circulated that their marriage failed because Mundia was involved with women but she came out to rubbish the claims.

    She maintained that she is straight and has never been in a relationship with a woman.
