    The burial of Zari Hassan's ex-hubby Ivan Ssemwanga came with much drama and pomp than sorrow.

    The tycoon was buried Tuesday, May 30, at his home in Kayunga, Uganda during the event attended by the who is who and socialites.

    Ivan died a week ago following a heart attack which was blamed on various things including loss of property to a conman.

    Zari and her three sons were at the burial where she was under tight security especially after being accused of having a hand in the death of her ex-husband.

    During the ceremony, members of the Rich Gang where Ivan was a leader popped champagne and threw bundles of money at his grave, after his casket was lowered to the grave.

    The popping of the champagne where some of it was poured inside the grave left many perplexed.
