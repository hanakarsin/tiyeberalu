    Phone maker confirms launch of Tecno Camon C9 in Kenya

    Tecno Mobile to soon launch its latest phone in the Camon series, Tecno C9, in Kenya A top company employee told TUKO.co.ke that the device will be the best camera phone in Africa Tecno C9 will retail at about KSh 20,000

    06 Apr 2024, 00:00

    – Tecno Mobile to soon launch its latest phone in the Camon series, Tecno C9, in Kenya

    – A top company employee told TUKO.co.ke that the device will be the best camera phone in Africa

    – Tecno C9 will retail at about KSh 20,000

    Tecno Mobile has confirmed a report that it is planning to launch a device billed as the greatest camera phone in Kenya.

    A senior executive at the company said the Tecno Camon C9 could be available in the country in June.

    The Tecno Camon C9's front camera is uniquely placed at the center of the screen. This will be exceptional for selfie lovers as the photos will appear straight, he said.

    READ ALSO: See the cool app that every Kenyan should have on their phones

    The Tecno Camon C9's front camera is speculated to have 13 megapixels with double flash, just like the rear camera.

    The phone, according to the executive, has a wide angle capture technology and enhanced Picture Noise Reduction capability that you can ever imagine.

    READ ALSO: Why you need a portable mobile phone charger

    He could not disclose the Tecno Camon C9 price but indicated it would be favourable for a device of its calibre. Tecno Camon C9 will cost about $200 (KSh 20,000), according to company sources.

    Tecno Camon C9 is the third device in the camera-centric Camon series after the Tecno Camon C8 and Tecno Camon C5.

    TUKO.co.ke had earlier published exclusive and confidential photos of the Tecno Camon C9 that an employee leaked.

    From the photos, one could tell that the Tecno C9 will be a great improvement to the predecessor - Tecno C8.

    Tecno C8, which was launched in Kenya in August 2015, currently goes for about KSh 10,000 – KSh 14,000.

    The marketing manager could still not disclose the Tecno Camon C9 features but the photos and Tecno C8 specifications give a hint.

    From the photos shared; one can tell that the Tecno Camon C9 still has the 13 megapixels rear camera with slight change in the flashes; they have been been merged.

    Camon C8 has a powerful 1.3GHz Quad-core processor. Our source had intimated that the Tecno Camon C9’s processor would be way much faster than this.

    Other specifications of the Camon C8 that one can expect to find (or improved) in the Tecno Camon C9 are: 16 GB ROM; 1 GB RAM; expandable MicroSD up to 128 GB; and a powerful 3000mAh battery.
