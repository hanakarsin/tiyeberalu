    Director of Ethics and Moral Foundation Ambaisi Masai has sensationally claimed that men who marry when they are financially stable are likely to settle with women who are seeking economic refuge. ‍

    According to him, most women are in marriages because they cannot handle life alone, but most men are in marriages because they love their wives.

    "If a man leaves, a woman will go to court. If a woman leaves, a man will fight alone," he said.

    The Financial Stability Tag

    He attributed his sentiments to the fact that most women look to settle with men who are financially stable, a requirement that tops many lists.

    On the contrary, when men are asked what they are looking for in women most mention love and loyalty.

    "Be cautious if you start dating when you are financially stable. Chances are very high that you will marry an economic refugee in the name of a wife," he maintained.

    He added that the more women continue being empowered, the less the chances of marriages surviving.

    "Marriages stand because men can provide. A marriage where a woman alone provides can never stand," he concluded.

    Women Insist Men Must Provide

    Interestingly, most women who commented embraced the tag of economic refugees, maintaining that men should continue providing for them as they only help where they can.

    Nyathi Dala:

    "Proudly an economic refugee. Men must provide and they become more lovely and handsome when they are financially stable and responsible. Dear men support more economic refugees and your marriages will stand like the stone rejected by the builders."

    Agribinah Mochache:

    "Whether a woman is financially stable or not, a man should always provide for that marriage to stand. A woman will only help when you can't."

    Mike Luther King Jnr:

    "To be sincere, no woman would like to suffer in marriage, particularly financially and there's no crown for suffering. If you find one who is stable stick with him,you will learn to love later."
