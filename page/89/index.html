NLC Reveals New Style For Attending Meetings With Government

17 May 2024, 00:00

The Nigeria Labour Congress (NLC) has declared that it would no longer attend meetings with the federal government on the eve of any planned action. This was made known on Wednesday by the NLC president, Joe Ajaero, during a press conference in Abuja. According to him, the government only uses meetings held on the eve of planned actions to delay their plans and mobilization of members in a bid to prevent them from carrying out their actions.
