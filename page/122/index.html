General Yakubu Gowon is a Nigerian military and political leader. He took power after the military coup d'état in 1966 and was overthrown in 1975. He was the 3rd Head of State of Nigeria, having been preceded by Major General Johnson Aguiyi-Ironsi and succeeded by General Murtala Mohammed. General Gowon served as Nigeria's Head of State from 1966 to 1975, when he was overthrown.
