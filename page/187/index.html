Again, Naira Loses Value Against Dollar

05 Feb 2024, 00:00

Nigeria’s currency, the Naira, lost more value against the United States (US) Dollar at the parallel market yesterday. Naija News understands that on Thursday, January 4, the Naira plummeted to ₦1,240 per US Dollar from ₦1,235 per dollar it exchanged for the previous day. However, the Naira was said to have appreciated to N895.23 against the US Dollar in the Nigerian Foreign Exchange Market (NAFEM), thus widening the gap between the official and parallel market exchange rates to N344.
