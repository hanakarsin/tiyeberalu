    15 Aug 2024, 00:00

    A young bride is said to have suffered a traumatic shock after her 40-year-old groom, Mohammed Zakari, died in a fatal accident on his way to their wedding at Fumesua on the Kumasi-Accra highway in Ghana.

    Naija News gathered that the unfortunate incident occurred on Sunday, March 13 around 1:00pm.

    According to the police report, the groom and four of his friends were on their way to his wedding venue in a Ford Escape vehicle with registration number AS 3222-21 when it lost control and ran into a ditch.

    Sadly, the groom died on the spot while the four other occupants in the vehicle were rushed to KNUST Hospital for medical care.

    One of the victims who sustained life-threatening injury has since been transferred to Komfo Anokye Teaching Hospital (KATH) for better treatment.

    The Ejisu Motor Transport and Traffic Directorate (MTTD), in a statement on the incident noted that it has commenced an  investigation towards the cause of the incident.

    The deceased was buried the next day, according to Islamic rites.
