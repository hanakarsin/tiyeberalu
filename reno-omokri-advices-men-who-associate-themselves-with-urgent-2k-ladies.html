    In other news, the family of 12-year-old Sylvester Oromoni has reacted to the decision of the court to grant suspects in the murder N1 million bail.

    Recall that the suspects who were involved in the alleged murder of the 12-year-old were granted a bail of 1million each on Tuesday, December 21st.

    The Chief Magistrate, Olatunbosun Adeola after hearing the bail application filed by the counsel of the defendants ordered that 2 sureties be provided in like sum. According to Daily Sun, the father of Sylvester questioned the decision of the judge to grant the suspects bail considering the fact that the 21 days the police requested for their investigation had not yet elapsed.

    He said: “Yesterday I was called that those boys were granted bail by a Magistrate Court in Yaba, Lagos. I said, “why?” I now called the Commissioner of Police, Lagos State and he told me; I was not aware, and that they were not done with the investigation.
