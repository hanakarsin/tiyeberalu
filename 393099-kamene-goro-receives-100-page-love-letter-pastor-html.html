    Kiss 100 FM presenter Kamene Goro was on Tuesday, November 24, left in stitches after receiving a love letter from a coast-based pastor.

    The vocal radio presenter received shared the hilarious news with her fans during her breakfast show with Jalang'o.

    In a video on their station's social media pages, Kamene was seeing reading the address on the brown envelope before opening to see what was inside.

    According to her, the pastor indicated the letter was extremely urgent and even got her station's address and her name right.

    According to the love letter, the pastor, known as Fred Tindi from Mtwapa, gave out his phone number to make it easier for her to reach him.

    The beauty showed off the 100-page letter which was handwritten.

    "Forget about the 100 pages. The man also put in newspaper cuttings. He also attached his CV and all his education certificates," revealed Kamene.

    In the letter, Tindi said he got saved in the year 1998 when the radio presenter was only six years.

    The man of God said he was "led by the Holy Spirit" in writing the 100-page letter.

    Below is the letter's introduction:

    "Dear Mitchelle, I take this opportunity to write to you this missive out of the blue but led by the Holy Spirit who is God and reveals to us the mind and heart of Jehovah God. First and foremost, I want you to know that I only got to hear about you first last year, then I saw you in the newspaper, then I saw you being interviewed by Amina on the Trend. I started my walk of salvation in 1998 when you were only six years."

    In other news, Kamene recently asked women to stop assuming girlfriend roles for men who have not officially claimed them.

    "Ladies, if he has not made it clear that you are his woman, please don't act like one. If he has not asked you, "will you be my girlfriend or will you be my woman, please do not act like one. Do not assume the position," she said.

    She encouraged them to have fun and flirt with whoever comes their way.

    "Go out there. Flirt with his friends, go out with his brothers, make his uncle your sponsor. You still do not belong to anyone," added Kamene.
