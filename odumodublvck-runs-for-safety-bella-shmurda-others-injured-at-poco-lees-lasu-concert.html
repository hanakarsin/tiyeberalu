    21 Apr 2024, 00:00

    The ‘Homecoming’ concert of Lagos State University (LASU) alumnus and popular dancer, Pascal Odinaka, popularly known as Poco Lee, ended abruptly on Thursday evening.

    Naija News understands that there was chaos at the concert venue yesterday, forcing the likes of rapper, Tochukwu Gbubemi Ojogwu, popularly known as Odumodublvck, to scamper for safety.

    It was gathered that some suspected cultists stormed the venue and disrupted happenings midway into the concert.

    Viral pictures and videos from the event shared on social media showed how popular singer LASU alumnus, Bella Shmurda, was rushed out.

    Many people allegedly sustained varying degrees of injuries in the incident which has caused stirs on social media.

    The host, Poco Lee, was seen lamenting about the incident in another video now viral on the internet.

    In the video, he could be heard saying, “What’s going on? How they [guest artists] wan take come back next time?

    “It’s not making sense. The whole artists I promised you, they are all here. But these guys [the hoodlums] don’t listen.”
