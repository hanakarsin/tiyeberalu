    Call girls camp in Bomet county eyeing brisk business with tea farmers

    - Major entertainment joints and lodgings in Bomet county have been booked by call girls - The farmers are expected to receive their tea bonuses soon - Area police boss warned locals against walking with large sums of money

    09 Jun 2024, 00:00

    - Major entertainment joints and lodgings in Bomet county have been booked by call girls

    In what appears as a pattern now, sex workers are said to be in Bomet county as they close their calendar year having registered brisk business with maize farmers in Trans Nzoia and Uasin Gishu counties, miraaa farmers in Meru county and wheat farmers in Narok county.

    Bomet county is known to be home to majority small scale tea farmers alongside neighboring Kericho.

    The farmers are expected to earn their tea bonuses soon and sex workers, being business minded, have turned to the sleepy town to make a killing.

    Skimpily dressed call girls are said to have booked lodgings and other entertainment spots in the town to take advantage of reckless and randy farmers likely to squander their tea bonuses in days.

    Small restaurants in nearby trading centers of Silibwet, Mogogosiek and Kapkwen are said to have a major business boost as the women have booked hotels and restaurants ready to trade their flesh.

    Bomet Central Police Commander Musa Imamai Omar cautioned local farmers against short-lived bliss and instead advised them to invest or spend the money wisely.

    He advised them not to carry large amounts of money around as they will be major targets for thieves who would want to milk them dry.

    In past years, during tea bonus payment, Bomet has been a hive of activities as locals engage in careless drinking and merry-making at the expense of their families.
