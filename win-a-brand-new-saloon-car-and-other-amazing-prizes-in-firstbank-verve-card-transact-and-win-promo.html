    Win A Brand-new Saloon Car And Other Amazing Prizes In FirstBank-Verve Card Transact And Win Promo

    16 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Don’t be left out as Nigerians are already cashing out big-time in the FirstBank and Verve card campaign with 50 million naira worth of prizes up for grabs.

    FirstBank, Nigeria’s leading financial inclusion services provider has partnered with Verve Card to reward customers for their continued usage of the Verve debit Card.

    The eight-week promo will end on Wednesday, 30 March 2022 as 2,601 customers will win various prizes. These prizes include: N20,000 Cash Prize, N10,000 Cash Prize, N10,000 worth of airtime; power generating sets, refrigerator, cooking gas and the grand prize of a brand-new car.

    The promo will reward customers on a weekly and monthly basis. In every week of the promo, 200 customers who carry out a minimum of 2 transactions with their Verve debit card stand a chance to win N10,000.00 worth of airtime, cash prizes of N10,000 and N20,000.

    Furthermore, 100 customers who perform a minimum of 8 transactions within 4 weeks with their Verve debit card will be rewarded with either a Generator set, Refrigerator, Gas Cooker or N50,000 cash prize every four weeks.

    A Grand prize of a brand-new car will also be won by a lucky customer who performs a minimum of 16 transactions during the campaign period.

    Since the promo began 500 people (100 customers weekly) have walked home with N10,000 cash – after they performed a minimum of 2 transactions weekly, while 500 people (100 customers weekly) won N10,000 worth of Airtime – with a minimum of 2 transactions weekly.

    Naija News reports that 500 people (100 customers weekly) also grabbed N20,000 cash – for performing a minimum of 4 transactions with their Verve cards weekly.

    While 50 customers won various household items worth N50,000 (Generator, table top refrigerator and Gas Cooker) for performing a minimum of 8 transactions in a month.

    The fun doesn’t stop there as loyal FirstBank customers still have 951 begging to be won in the next three weeks including the 2022 Suzuki S-Presso car.

    The break down of prizes up for grabs include:

    300 people to win 10,000 cash

    300 customers to win N10,000 worth of airtime

    300 customers to win N20,000 cash

    50 customers to win various household items (Generator, table top refrigerator and Gas Cooker) for the second month N50,000.

    1 customer to win the star prize of a brand new 2022 Suzuki S-Presso car for transacting 16 times during the period of the promo (8 Weeks)

    Speaking on the promo, FirstBank’s Group Executive, e-Business & Retail Products, Chuma Ezirim said the promo reinforces the value the financial institution places on its customers.

    He said: “We are happy with the ongoing verve debit card promo designed to appreciate customers for their continued usage and adoption of the card for various digitally-driven transactions.

    “With the promo being instrulmental to reinforcing the value placed by customers on its versatile capabilities, we encourage the continued usage of the verve card as it is a card offering that promotes safe, convenient and rewarding digital banking service to customers.”

    Naija News reports that the Verve debit card is a Naira-denominated debit card that offers a convenient, fast, and secured payment for goods and services.

    It is accepted on all channels connected to the Verve International network where the Interswitch/Verve logo is displayed; ATMs, POS and Web in Nigeria.

    The Bank’s Verve debit Cards make up a notable percentage of the industry’s base. Hence, this Promo is aimed at appreciating our cardholders while growing the bank’s card adoption and usage.

    Visit the nearest FirstBank branch today to pick up your Verve card and start getting rewarded!
